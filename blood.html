How To Donate Blood

how_to_reg

1. Registration

Donor must fill out the comprehensive registration form with accurate details.

medical_information

2. Medical Check-up

A brief medical check-up (approx. 5 mins) including medical history, lifestyle questions, temperature, blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin test.

bloodtype

3. Donation

A trained phlebotomist draws a unit (350ml/450ml) of blood. This process typically takes about 8-10 minutes.

local_cafe

4. Refreshment

Enjoy light refreshments for about 10 minutes to help your body recover and rehydrate.
